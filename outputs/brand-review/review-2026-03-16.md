## Standing Brand Flags (Recurring Reminders for Content Writer)

These patterns have not appeared in this sprint but are flagged for ongoing vigilance:

1. **"Help you..."** — This is a consultant's phrase. CFA installs things. Replace with "install," "build," "run," "fix."
2. **Passive voice in proof claims** — "Results were seen" / "improvements were made" — always rewrite active: "We cut estimate time by 60%."
3. **The enthusiasm opener** — Any draft that begins with "Excited to share..." or "Big news..." is auto-rejected.
4. **Over-explaining the mechanism** — If a post needs 3 paragraphs to explain what the agent does, it's trying to teach instead of sell. Cut. Proof + CTA. The mechanism is for demos.
