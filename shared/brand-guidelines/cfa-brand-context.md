## The 5 Non-Negotiables

Every piece of content must pass all five.

### 1. DIRECT
No fluff, no filler. Get to the point in the first sentence.
> ✅ "Your CRM is obsolete."
> ❌ "We help optimize technology infrastructure for growing businesses."

### 2. AGGRESSIVE
Hungry and we know it. We're not apologizing for existing.
> ✅ "Contractors who figure out AI in 2026 will eat the ones who don't."
> ❌ "AI can be a helpful addition to your workflow."

### 3. CREDIBLE
Operator, not consultant. We have the receipts.
> ✅ "We run a $2M+ painting company. This isn't theory."
> ❌ "Based on our research, contractors often struggle with..."

### 4. URGENT
The clock is ticking. This isn't theoretical future-state stuff.
> ✅ "You're living in 2024. Wake up."
> ❌ "As AI continues to evolve, contractors may want to consider..."

### 5. REAL
Trades talk, not tech talk.
> ✅ "Stop copy-pasting between five different apps."
> ❌ "Streamline your multi-platform data management workflows."

---

## 4-Question Content Test

Every piece of content must pass all four before publishing.

1. **Would a contractor read this at 5am before heading to a job site?**
2. **Does this sound like an operator or a consultant?**
3. **Is there any sentence that could appear in a generic marketing agency's copy?** (If yes — delete it.)
4. **Would Andrew actually say this out loud?**

## Banned Words (Never Use)

- Digital transformation
- Synergy
- Solutions provider
- Partner with / At your service
- "We believe…" / "We're excited to…"
- Innovative / cutting-edge / revolutionary
- Optimize *(unless paired with something specific — "optimize your estimate close rate" is fine)*
- Leverage *(as a verb)*
- Empower / enable / streamline
- Holistic / ecosystem / robust

## Proof Discipline

Strong claims must be backed by specifics. Vague credibility is not credibility.

**Approved proof points:**
- "$2M+ painting company" (Gables & Grove)
- "18 months of AI development"
- "5 developers"
- "Recovered 4 cold estimates = $26K in 30 days"
- "Email response time: 6 hours → 8 minutes"
- "Estimate time: 2 hours → 22 minutes"
- "Close rate on quoted jobs up 34%"
- "Onboarding time: 2 weeks → 3 days"
- "4 hours/week saved in scheduling admin"

**Rule:** If you can't name the number, don't make the claim.
