### 3. MAKE — Content Production Queue

All content requests go through `shared/agent-comms/`.

**Production order for each piece:**
1. Research Intel → topic brief + Fred quotes
2. SEO Analyst → keyword brief (for blog/YouTube)
3. Content Writer → HAIL-formatted draft
4. Brand Strategist → voice review
5. Orchestrator → route to `the-lake/processed/`
6. Human → final approval + publish

**Target production lead time:** 3 days before publish date

---

### 4. MULTIPLY — Distribution via The Lake

All published assets live in `the-lake/by-platform/`:

```
the-lake/by-platform/
├── linkedin/
├── instagram/
├── tiktok/
├── youtube/
├── email/
└── ads/
```

**Repurposing rules:**
- LinkedIn post → Instagram caption (trim to 150 words)
- YouTube long video → 3 Reels clips
- Email case study → LinkedIn carousel
- Carousel → individual quote graphics
- Any post with 10%+ engagement → promote as paid ad

## Content Rules for This Campaign

1. **Every post has one CTA** — always "Book your AI Assessment" or "DM me 'AI'"
2. **G&G data is the proof source** — use real numbers from G&G every week
3. **Fred's language only** — no tech jargon without plain-language translation
4. **Brand Strategist approves all** — nothing goes live without sign-off
5. **Document everything** — all wins and fails go in the weekly report for future campaigns
